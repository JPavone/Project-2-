About

  1. How does it work?

    2. Felines for a Free America will help you register to vote, and update your voter registration. We’ll also help you get absentee ballots and vote be mail.

  2. Can I vote online?

    3. Unfortunately, you still have to mark a paper ballot and mail it in.

  3. Am I eligible to vote?

    4. You must be a U.S. citizen and at least 18 by the next general election. If you have been convicted of a felony and have completed serving a court-ordered sentence, you are eligible to register to vote.

  4. Do I have to affiliate with a political party?

    5. No. If you choose not to register with a party, you will be registered as “unaffiliated”and able to vote in any nonpartisan primary election held in your jurisdiction.
